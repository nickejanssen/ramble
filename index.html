ABOUT OUR TOUR

Highlights and Hotspots

Your experience will include an entertaining 90-minute rambling stroll with a licensed professional guide along Savannah's famous River Street.

Savannah is steeped in fascinating history, and we will explore Georgia's first city as we walk over 200 year old cobblestones, where King Cotton was loaded on ships in years gone past. Century old buildings that once housed priceless cotton are now converted into eclectic shops, art galleries, casual and upscale restaurants and quaint pubs. You may enjoy a beverage at popular local establishments such as: Lizzy's Tequila Bar, Kevin Barry's Irish Pub or Treehouse. Your tour concludes at the popular Treehouse (Savannah Magazine's winner for Best Late-Night Spot) where your wristband (included in the price of the tour) will give you VIP entrance and an all night pass to this Savannah hot spot.

Beverages and gratuities are not included in the price of the tour. Everyone must be 21 or older to participate. If consuming alcohol please bring your ID as bars in Savannah will card you regardless of age.

ABOUT OUR STOPS

Lizzy's Tequila Bar

Our first stop is housed in an 1800’s trade warehouse, this 2 story waterfront location has a diverse menu showcasing true Tex-Mex favorites and includes margaritas and craft beer from their full service bar.

Kevin Barry's Irish Pub

Rambling on, we will find ourselves at Kevin Barry's, a traditional Irish Pub that is ranked in American's Best & Top 10 Irish Pubs. They were also recognized as one of the top military bars in the world by Military.com.

Treehouse

Your Ramble concludes in Savannah’s famous City Market with the Grand Finale at Treehouse, winner of Savannah Magazine’s “BEST LATE NIGHT SPOT”. Your wristband gives you all night VIP access so grab a drink and head out to the balconies to enjoy the best DJ’s and live music or you can stay inside and hit the dance floor!

Grand Finale

When our tour concludes in Savannah’s Historic City Market it’s time to put that wristband you received at the beginning of the tour to use. Your wristband will give you all night VIP access to Treehouse, so skip around that line and climb on up for a drink at Savannah’s Best Late Night Spot. The modern bar atmosphere has a professional bartending staff, live music, DJ's, dancing and offers a professional JBL sound system, as well as his and hers nicely appointed bathrooms. If you would like to grab a bite in between drinks, City Market is at the foot of Treehouse with many options for food. But remember that VIP wristband lets you skip around that line for the Treehouse all night long, so grab a bite and then swing on back to Treehouse. Look forward to seeing you soon!
